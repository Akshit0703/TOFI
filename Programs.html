YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM

It aims to bring out the leadership qualities of the YOUTH.

The managerical positions are filled on the basis of merit. Involvement in decision making is priortised as "leadership can only be effective when there is participation."

They experience the cultural and universal diversity through youth forums , city chapters, clusters, state and national conferences

Currently there are four projects that require youth's attention and solutions:

  • Education
  • Food and Shelter
  • Sustainable Development and Environment Issues
  • Illegal and Gender bias Issues

"YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM" exists for the youth to act in unison with an aim to create an impact by addressing social, political, and environmental issues. It makes them realize significance of a youth led nation .
